Oaths & Blessings

Sacred Texts of the Forest Guardian

These scrolls and documents contain the sacred words that guide and empower the Forest Guardian. They may be printed and displayed as reminders of your noble duty.

The Forest Guardian's Oath

I, Guardian of this sacred space between wild and tame, do solemnly swear:

To watch with gentle eyes and listen with patient ears,

To move with care upon the earth that holds all life,

To protect those smaller and more vulnerable than myself,

To honor the wisdom of nature in all its forms.

I promise to remember:

That each creature has a purpose and a place,

That patience reveals what haste conceals,

That the smallest beings often carry the greatest magic.

I pledge:

To learn the language of rustling leaves and twitching whiskers,

To record what I observe with honesty and wonder,

To share the beauty I witness without disturbing its delicate balance.

As Guardian, I understand:

I am not master but caretaker,

Not conqueror but companion,

Not owner but humble observer of miracles unfolding.

This oath I take freely and with full heart,

In service to the bunnies and all forest beings,

From this day forward.

Forest Guardian Mellington
Under the witness of moon and stars

The Guardian's Blessing

May the forest paths welcome your footsteps,

May the wild things recognize your gentle heart,

May your eyes be keen to beauty often overlooked,

And your ears attuned to the whispers of green growing things.

When morning dew gathers on spider silk,

May you witness this small miracle.

When twilight brings the day's last birdsong,

May you be present to receive this gift.

Let your hands touch the earth with reverence,

Let your voice be soft among the trees,

Let your heart beat in rhythm with the seasons' dance,

Let your spirit find home in the in-between places.

Blessed be the Guardian who stands watch,

Who protects without possessing,

Who observes without interfering,

Who loves without limiting.

May your purple glitter shine in moments of need,

May your bunny friends multiply and thrive,

May your guardian heart find joy in each season,

And peace in the knowledge that you belong to this place,

As surely as it belongs to you.

The Forest Spirits
Whispered on a midsummer breeze

Forest Guardian License

Official Certificate of Guardianship

This certifies that

MELLINGTON

has been officially recognized and appointed as

GUARDIAN OF BUNNIES AND BEARER OF PURPLE GLITTER

with all rights, responsibilities, and privileges thereof.

The bearer of this license is authorized to:

  • Observe and document bunny activities within the designated forest area
  • Maintain and protect natural habitats conducive to bunny welfare
  • Deploy purple glitter in emergency situations (use sparingly)
  • Interpret bunny communications for the uninitiated
  • Bestow honorary bunny names upon worthy forest visitors
  • Access the secret Bunny Archives (location disclosed at midnight ceremonies only)

This license is granted in recognition of demonstrated patience, compassion, observational skills, and a proven ability to remain perfectly still while bunnies are present.

Approved

This license cannot be transferred, duplicated, or used to avoid paying taxes.
